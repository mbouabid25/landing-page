About Marwa Bouabid

Marwa Bouabid is a dynamic and innovative professional with a rich multicultural background and experience in computer science and mathematics. Raised in Morocco, Marwa attended a French school from elementary to secondary education, which provided her with an understanding of diverse cultures and languages. This unique upbringing has endowed her with exceptional social skills and the ability to communicate effectively in five languages: French, Moroccan Arabic, Modern Standard Arabic, English, and Spanish.

Marwa’s academic journey is marked by excellence and dedication. She pursued a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Mathematics at Mount Holyoke College, excelling in courses such as Data Structures, Operating Systems, Algorithms, and Software Design and Development. Her outstanding performance earned her several honors, including the Gina Jacobsen Prize for Excellence in Mathematics and an NSF award for a Summer REU on Artificial Intelligence for Computational Creativity at Brown University. This award highlights her keen interest and significant contributions to the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Marwa’s passion for AI is further demonstrated in her professional experiences. At Brown University, she worked as an AI and VR Researcher, where she was selected by Google to build 3D tools and virtual settings to enhance visual experiences for the visually impaired. Her project, which involved training models using the MS-COCO dataset to generate real-time image-captioning of art pieces, ranked third among all projects at the Brown University Undergraduate Research Symposium. This role allowed Marwa to merge her interests in AI and accessibility, showcasing her commitment to leveraging technology for social good.

In addition to her research experience, Marwa has made significant contributions as a Mobile and iOS Developer at Valence Vibrations, where she led the development of an iOS app using Swift, aimed at improving emotional perception for individuals with physical impairments. Her technical prowess was further demonstrated through her project management role at Click App, where she developed a sports matching app with a full stack, implemented CI/CD pipelines, and optimized the system for fast search results using Redis caching.

Marwa’s leadership and mentorship skills are evident from her roles at Mount Holyoke College, where she has been a Computer Science Mentor and Tutor, helping over 100 students succeed despite the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. She also managed class social media channels, organized events for over 800 students, and led initiatives that fostered a supportive academic community.

With a passion for technology and a commitment to making a positive impact, Marwa Bouabid continues to pursue excellence in all her endeavors. Her ability to navigate and bridge different cultures, coupled with her technical skills and multilingual abilities, make her a valuable asset in any professional setting. Whether she is developing innovative solutions, conducting cutting-edge research, or leading teams, Marwa’s contributions are marked by her dedication, intelligence, and collaborative spirit. Her keen interest in Artificial Intelligence drives her to constantly explore new frontiers in technology, aiming to create solutions that are not only innovative but also socially impactful.
