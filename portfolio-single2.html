What this is

Built this personal website from scratch in HTLM/CSS. I got inspiration from a Bootstrap template but changed 90% of the design and functionality. I inserted images, added animations (Using JavaScript) changed the structure of tables, added buttons and shadows, modified the navbar and more! I also bought my own domain name and took care of the back end portion of this website.

What it does

On this website, you can find information about me and my work. On the homepage you will find my resume and my linkedin, as well as a portfolio featuring some of my projects. If you click on one of them, you will find a detailed description of each project with a short descriptive video or links to see it live. My website also links to my Medium blog where I write about my journey learning engineering and product management.
